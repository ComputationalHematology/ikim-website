IKIM – the Institute for Artificial Intelligence in Medicine

Founded in 2019 the Institute for Artificial Intelligence in Medicine (IKIM) brings together researchers from a number of disciplines to perform research, improve patient care and innovate training of tomorrow’s physicians. The institute is part of the University Medicine Essen and the University of Duisburg-Essen.
